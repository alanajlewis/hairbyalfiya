About Alfiya

A familiar face,
a trusted pair of hands

Alfiya has been crafting beautiful hair in Finchley for over a decade, building a loyal following who return not just for the results — but for the whole experience. A space where you're listened to, never hurried, and always leave feeling like yourself at your best.

Now based at Harry's Hair & Beauty on Regent's Park Road, she brings the same warmth and precision to every appointment. From a classic cut to a full colour transformation, every visit is tailored entirely to you.
